Crypto-Dash

This is an Android application developed for the purpose of tracking the values of over 2000 crypto-currencies. This app was developed as my senior project in undergrad.

The senior project class at UNCG was meant to simulate an enterprise software development environment. Four project members, including Logan Stanfield, Max Bushman, John Kaiserlik, and myself agreed on a modern application that could display crypto-currencies real-time values. Working with these other students solidified my motivation to continue into the software development field. I love working in teams where communication and teamwork are a priority.

My task was to build an app preferences screen, as well as a tutorial page to provide the basics of investing in crypto-currecies. Logan Stanfield worked on displaying the stock graph and values with other information on the dashboard page. John Kaiserlik developed the crypto-currency live news feed page. And Max Bushman handled the APIs that allowed us to get all of this information to the app.
