Family Tree

This program was my first encounter with AI. My goal was to produce a Prolog console program that would give me information on my family tree given a relationship and a list of names that fit that relationship. At the time, Alpha GO had just defeated the world's best GO player so expectations were high for my program. I am sure I could made something just as great if I had some extra time, but semesters are short. And now with Starcraft 1 AI and machine learning becoming the next challenge, it feels like an impossible task to live up to the AI standards in such a short amount of time by myself.
